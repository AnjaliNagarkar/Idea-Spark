Section 2: FAQs

Find answers to frequently asked questions here.

1.How do I log in to my student account?
Visit the student login page on our website and enter your username and password in the designated fields.


2.What should I do if I forgot my password?
You can typically reset your password by clicking on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Follow the prompts to reset your password using your registered email address or other verification methods.


3.Who should I contact if I encounter technical issues with the student portal?
If you encounter technical issues with the student portal, you can usually contact the IT help desk or support team for assistance. They can provide troubleshooting tips and help resolve any issues you may be experiencing.


4. How do I reset my password?
If you've forgotten your password or need to reset it, follow these steps: Click on the 'Forgot Password?' link on the login page. Enter your registered email address. Follow the instructions sent to your email to reset your password.


5. Who should I contact for technical support?
For technical assistance or further questions, please contact our support team at [support@email.com] or [phone number].


6. I'm having trouble submitting a reservation request, What should I do?
If you encounter issues while submitting a reservation request, try the following troubleshooting steps: Ensure you are logged in to your student account. Clear your browser cache and cookies, then try again. Use a different browser or device to submit the request. Contact technical support if the problem persists.


7. How can I submit a machine reservation request?
To submit a reservation request, follow these steps: Log in to your student account on the website. Navigate to the 'Inbox' section. Send your reservation request through the form provided in the inbox section Submit the request for admin approval.


8. How do I track the status of my reservation request?
When you submit a machine reservation request, you will receive email notifications regarding the status of your request


9. How many reservations can I make at once?
Students can typically make one reservation at a time per machine. If you need to reserve multiple machines or time slots, please submit separate requests for each.


10. I'm having trouble submitting a reservation request, What should I do?
If you encounter issues while submitting a reservation request, try the following troubleshooting steps: Ensure you are logged in to your student account. Clear your browser cache and cookies, then try again. Use a different browser or device to submit the request. Contact technical support if the problem persists.
